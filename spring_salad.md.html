Spring Farro and Veggie Salad

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup farro
  • salt
  • 6 oz sugar snap peas halfed diagonally
  • 1 bunch asparagus cut into 2 in pieces diagonally
  • 1/2 small red apple thinly sliced
  • 2 Tbsp basil finely chopped
  • 1 scallion finely chopped
  • 1 Tbsp cilantro finely chopped
  • 1 Tbsp parsley finely
  • 1/4 cup feta crumbled

Dressing

  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 Tbsp honey
  • 1 Tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil 1 cup of water with 2 tsp salt and add farro, cooking covered on medium low 18-20 minutes
  2. Drain and rinse farro with cold water
  3. Combine all dressing ingredients in a small bowl
  4. Boil snap peas and asparagus (add 2 tsp salt) for 2 minutes, then transfer veggies to an ice bath
  5. In salad bowl, combine farro, veggies, apple slices, basil, scallion, cilantro and parsley
  6. Toss salad with dressing and add feta

Note

  • Would be good with chicken
