    Moussaka

    Ingredients

    • 2 large eggplants

    • salt

    • olive oil

    • 4 Tbs breadcrumbs

    • 1 large yellow onion chopped finely

    • 1 lb ground beef

    • 1 Tbs dried oregano

    • 1 tsp cinnamon

    • 0.5 tsp pepper

    • 0.5 tsp nutmeg

    • 0.5 tsp paprika

    • 0.5 cup red wine

    • 14 oz can diced tomatoes

    • 1 tsp sugar

    • 0.5 cup hot beef broth

    • 1/3 cup and 2 Tbsp olive oil

    • 2/3 cup flour

    • 1/2 tsp salt

    • 1/4 tsp nutmeg

    • 4 cups milk warmed

    • 2 eggs

    Instructions

    1. Cut eggplants into 1/4 in slices and salt to de-bitter. Let sit for 30 minutes and then pat dry.

    2. Brush sheet pans with olive oil, put eggplants onto sheet pans, brush eggplant with olive oil

    3. Broil until soft and golden brown

    4. In a skillet, heat 2 Tbsp olive oil and cook the onions

    5. Add the beef and cook, draining fat afterwards

    6. Stir in oregano, cinnamon, pepper, nutmeg, paprika

    7. Add wine and boil for 1 minute to reduce

    8. Stir in tomato, sugar and broth

    9. Simmer on medium-low for 20-30 minutes

    10. Heat oven to 350

    11. In a saucepan, heat olive oil on medium-high until shimmering

    12. Stir in flour, salt and pepper and cook until golden

    13. While wisking, slowly add milk

    14. Cook for 6 minutes stirring occasionally

    15. Add nutmeg

    16. In a separate bowl, take some of the mixture and stir with eggs

    17. Add mixture back to bechamel and wisk, bringing to a boil for 2 minutes

    18. Remove from heat to cool

    19. In an oiled 9.5" x 13" pan layer eggplant, meat sauce, more eggplant, bechamel, bread crumbs

    20. Bake for 45 minutes (can broil for a bit for golden brown color)

    21. Let sit for 10 before serving
