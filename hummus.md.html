    Hummus

    Ingredients

    • 1/2 cup tahini
    • 1/3 cup olive oil
    • 1 clove garlic roughly chopped
    • 3 tbsp lemon juice
    • kosher salt
    • 31 oz chickpeas cans drained, liquid reserved
    • za'atar for serving

    Instructions

    1. Combine tahini, olive oil, garlic, lemon juice and 2 tsp salt in a food processor
    2. Puree until smooth
    3. Add chickpeas and continue pureeing until a chunky paste
    4. Slowly add (1/3 to 1/2 cup) reserved liquid to loosen hummus
    5. Process until whipped and creamy
    6. Over a large bowl, press the hummus through a sieve with a spatula
    7. Serve hummus with olive oil and za'atar
