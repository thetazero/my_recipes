    Cheese Bourekas

    Ingredients

    • 2 sheets frozen puff pastry
    • 1 2/3 cup feta cheese (good when some is replaced by mozarella too)
    • 2 large eggs
    • 2/3 cup ricotta cheese
    • salt and pepper
    • 1 large egg yolk

    Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 350 F
    2. In a bowl, mix all cheese, 2 eggs, and a pinch of salt and pepper
    3. On a lightly floured surface, roll out puff pastry sheet to a 12x12 square (I've done ~16x16 too) then cut into 4"x4" squares
    4. Place filling in center of each dough square
    5. Fold one corner of the dough across to create a triangle.
    6. Crimp the edges of the triangles with a form to seal shut
    7. Line baking sheet with parchment paper and place bourekas on baking sheet.
    8. In a separate bowl, wisk together egg yolk and 2 tsp of water to brush onto the tops of the bourekas
    9. Bake until golden brown and cooked through (about 30 minutes)
