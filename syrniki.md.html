    Syrniki (Sasha's recipe)

    Ingredients

    • 1 egg

    Instructions

    1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl
    2. Form mixture into small patties and place in pan on low heat
    3. Do not move the syrniki until the bottom has solidified
    4. When the bottom is browned, flip
    5. When cooked, remove from pan and start the next batch
