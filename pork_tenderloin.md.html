    Pork Tenderloin

    Simple pork tenderloin recipe for the oven, "it's okay"

    Ingredients

    • Pork tenderloin
    • Olive oil
    • 2-3 Tbs citrus juice (lemon, lime, or orange)
    • 2 tsp Italian seasoning
    • 1 tsp garlic powder
    • 1 tsp cumin
    • 1 tsp chili powder
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1/2 tsp paprika
    • 1/4 tsp black pepper

    Instructions

    1. Preheat oven to 400
    2. On a baking tray, rub olive oil on all sides of the pork and pierce in multiple locations with a fork
    3. Mix together seasonings and rub on pork
    4. Drizzle citrus juice on top
    5. Bake for 25-35 minutes (inside is 145)
    6. Spoon juices from meat over the pork and let sit for 5-10 minutes
