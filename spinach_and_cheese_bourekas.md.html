    Spinach and Cheese Bourekas

    Ingredients

    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 small yellow onion finely chopped
    • salt
    • 10 oz frozen spinach thawed and squeezed of excess water
    • 2 egg lightly beaten (separate)
    • 1 clove garlic minced
    • 1/8 tsp nutmeg
    • 1/3 cup ricotta
    • 1/3 cup grated mozzarella
    • 1/4 tsp pepper
    • 2 sheets frozen puff pastry thawed
    • sesame seeds

    Instructions

    1. Cook onion in olive oil over medium heat until lightly browned
    2. In bowl, combine onion, spinach, egg, garlic, nutmeg, ricotta, mozzarella, pepper and salt (1/2 tsp)
    3. Set oven to 350 C and grease 2 large baking sheets
    4. On a lightly floured surface, unroll the puff pastry rolling it into a 12 in square
    5. Cut into 4 in squares
    6. Spoon ~2 tbsp filling near the corner of each piece and flatten with a spoon
    7. Fold squares into triangles and press the edges to seal
    8. On the baking sheets, brush the tops with beaten egg nd sprinkle with sesame seeds
    9. Bake 30-35 minutes until puffed and golden brown
