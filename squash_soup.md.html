    Butternut Squash Soup

    Ingredients

    • olive oil
    • 1 large onion, chopped
    • salt
    • 1 butternut squash (3 lbs), peeled, seeded, cubed
    • 3 garlic cloves
    • 1 tsp sage
    • 1/2 tsp rosemary
    • 1 tsp grated fresh ginger
    • 3-4 cups vegetable broth
    • pepper

    Instructions

    1. In a large pot, saute onion, salt and pepper over medium heat until soft
    2. Add squash to pot and cook until it begins to soften
    3. Add garlic through ginger and cook 30 seconds or until fragarent
    4. Add borth, bring to a boil, cover, reduce heat to a simmer
    5. Cook 20-30 minutes until the squash is tender
    6. Let cool a bit, then pour the soup (in batches) into a blender and blend until smooth. Add more broth if soup is too thick.
    7. If soup has cooled too much, transfer it back to the stove for a bit
