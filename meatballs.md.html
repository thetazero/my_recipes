    Meatballs

    Ingredients

    • 1 lb any ground meat
    • 1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
    • 1 egg
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 2 cloves garlic
    • 1/2 cup parmesan
    • season with italian herbs, garlic powder

    Instructions

    1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl
    2. Transfer to baking sheet and bake for 10 minutes
