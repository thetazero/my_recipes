Israeli Couscous

Serves 6-8

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup sliced almonds
  • 3 1/2 cups broth
  • 4 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 3 cups israeli couscous
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 6 shallots (medium) halfed lengthwise and thinly sliced
  • kosher salt and pepper
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • parsley for serving

Instructions

  1. Toast almonds in pan over medium heat (5-7 minutes)
  2. Bring broth and thyme to boil
  3. Stir in couscous, turn heat to low, cover
  4. Cook 8-12 minutes until liquid is absorbed
  5. Remove from heat, keep covered for 5 minutes
  6. Cook shallots with oil in a pan until soft and browned
  7. Add shallots, almonds and lemon juice and oil to couscous
  8. Season with salt and pepper
  9. Top with parsley
