Plov

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 medium onions finely chopped
  • 2 1/2 lb beef chuck cut into 3/4 in chunks
  • 5 medium carrots peeled ad grated
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 5 garlic cloves finely chopped
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 5 cups water
  • 2 1/2 cups rice
  • 1/4 barberries (or currants)

Instructions

  1. In a large heavy bottomed pot, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat
  2. Add onions
  3. Once onions are lightly browned, add beef and cook until browned (7-10 minutes)
  4. Add carrots and cook until softened (3-5 minutes)
  5. Add salt (1 1/2 tsp), pepper (1/2 tsp), paprika, cumin, turmeric, coriander, garlic, and bay leaves stirring until fragrant
  6. Stir in 2 cups water, bring to a simmer
  7. Cover, turn heat to low, cook 40-45 minutes
  8. Pour rice and salt (1 tsp), and then 3 cups water into beef mixture (don't stir)
  9. Bring water to a boil, then bring back down to a simmer
  10. Cook uncovered for 10-12 minutes
  11. Poke holes in mixture
  12. Sprinkle berries over the top, cover and turn heat to low
  13. Cook 15-20 minutes
