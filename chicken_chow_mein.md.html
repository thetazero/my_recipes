    Chicken Chow Mein

    Ingredients

    Sauce

    • 1/4 cup oyster sauce
    • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
    • 1 Tbsp + 1 tsp sesame oil
    • 2 tsp brown sugar
    • pepper

    Noodles

    • 14 oz package chow mein noodles (fresh ramen or yakisoba can be substitued)
    • 2 Tbsp avocado oil
    • 3 boneless skinless chicken thighs cut into 1/2 in strips
    • 1/2 tsp salt
    • 3 garlic cloves minced
    • 1 tsp ginger grated
    • 1 medium carrot cut into 1/4 in rounds
    • 1 stalk celery cut into 1/4 in pieces
    • 1/4 medium yellow onion sliced
    • 2 green part of scallions cut into 2 in pieces
    • 2 baby bok choy cut into 1 in pieces
    • cilantro for garnish

    Instructions

    1. Mix sauce ingredients and wisk until sugar disolves
    2. In a large pot, pour an inch or two of water and set a steamer basket in the bottom. Bring the water to a boil and add noodles. Steam 3-5 minutes until slightly undercooked.
    3. In a wok, add avocado oil and cook chicken seasoned with salt
    4. After 2 minutes add garlic and ginger
    5. After 2-3 minutes add carrot, celery, onion,scallion and bok choy
    6. After 3 minutes add noodles and sauce, mixing as you cook for another 3 minutes
