Eggplant Parm Single Rounds

Ingredients

  • 1 eggplant cut into 1/4-1/2 in slices
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup parmesan
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs beaten
  • 1 Tbsp oil

For topping

  • tomato sauce
  • mozzarella
  • basil

Instructions

  1. Salt eggplant and let sit for 30 minutes before patting them dry
  2. Preheat oven to 425 F
  3. Combine breadcrumbs, parmesan, and seasoning.
  4. Dredge eggplant slices in flour, then egg, then breadcrumb mixture
  5. Place eggplant on baking sheet then brush each side with oil
  6. Bake for 15 minutes, flip, then bake for 15 more
  7. Top eggplant with sauce and mozzarella then bake for 10 more minutes
