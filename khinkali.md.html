Khinkali

Ingredients

Dough

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup water

Filling

  • 1 pound ground beef (can also use lamb, veal, pork or mixture)
  • 1/2 cup scallion greens chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • black pepper

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, salt and water

  2. Knead dough on a lightly floured surface

  3. Seal dough in a plastic bag for 30 minutes to 6 hours

  4. Mix all of the filling ingredients and set aside

  5. Half an hour before serving, boil water and add salt

  6. Cover and simmer

  7. On a lightly floured surface, cut dough into 4 pieces

  8. Flatten into a thin oval about 10 inches long

  9. Cut out circles (d=4 in)

  10. Place a tablespoon of filling in the center of a circle

  11. Pull up some edge, and then more next to it, pleating the second edge over the first

  12. Continue around the circle

  13. TO seal knot, pinch top 1/2 in below the top edge and twist

  14. After first two batches are made, bring water to a boil

  15. Place dumplings in the water

  16. Take dumplings out a minute after they have floated to the top (6-8 minutes)
