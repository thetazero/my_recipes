    Chicken Schnitzel

    Ingredients

    • 1 cup flour
    • 3 eggs lightly beaten
    • 2 cups panko breadcrumbs
    • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
    • kosher salt
    • freshly ground black pepper
    • vegetable oil(don't use avacado oil stupid) for frying

    Instructions

    1. Place the flour, eggs, and bread crumbs in three seperate shallow bowls.
    2. Butterfly the chicken breasts by slicing them in half horizontally.
    3. Use a meat pounder to pound the chicken breasts to 1/4 inch thickness.
    4. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
    5. Dredge the chicken breasts in the flour, then the eggs, and then the bread crumbs.
    6. Heat 1/4 inch of oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    7. Fry the chicken breasts until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-6 minutes per side.
    8. Transfer the chicken breasts to a paper towel-lined plate to drain.
